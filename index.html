About My culture

The Philippines is an archipelago in Southeast Asia made up of more than 7,000 islands, known for its tropical climate, beautiful beaches, and rich biodiversity. It is located in the western Pacific Ocean and has a mix of mountains, rainforests, and coastal areas that support many different plant and animal species.

The Philippines has a diverse culture influenced by Indigenous traditions as well as Spanish and American history. Its capital city is Manila, and the country is known for its festivals, music, food, and the strong sense of community among its people. Due to its many volcanoes, the country can generate natural energy from underground heat. The famous Mayon Volcano is known for its almost perfectly symmetrical cone shape, but it is also one of the most active volcanoes in the country. Despite natural disasters like typhoons and earthquakes, Filipinos are known worldwide for their resilience and strong sense of community, often called “bayanihan.”
